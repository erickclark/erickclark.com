    Portfolio

    Datafiniti

    Datafiniti crawls the web and compiles it into a massive database. The data is then sold to companies who use it to learn competitive price information, find sales leads, to do reputation management, and more.

    Wireframes

    One of my first projects at Datafiniti was to design the search interface for the company's massive database. I went though several sets of wireframes before I came to a design solution that worked well. Datafiniti was a new company at this point, and there were no existing customers to get feedback from, but I was able to review the wireframes with a group of potential customers.

    Finished Design

    After an initial version of the Datafiniti site was completed, I again sought feedback from potential customers. Their input was invaluable in showing not only where design improvements could be made, but also where new features could be added. I also set up Google Analytics and KissMetrics to track user behavior and key events on the site.

    Datafiniti Marketing Site

    Along with designing the Datafiniti search UI I also designed the company's marketing site. The process of designing this site went much quicker than designing the search interface. I went straight into working with colors and layouts, bypassing the sketching and wireframing stages. I worked with Datafiniti's marketing staff and quickly iterated though several layouts and visual design concepts before finding one that stakeholders approved of. Though it was not the process I normally use, we were able to get a site launched in a matter of weeks. Shown here are mobile and desktop views of a few pages from the site.
