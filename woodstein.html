The Woodward & Bernstein Watergate Papers

In February of 2005 The Harry Ransom Center at the University of Texas at Austin held an exhibition of The Woodward & Bernstein Watergate Papers. The papers were acquired by The Harry Ransom Center from the famed journalists Bob Woodward and Carl Bernstein. The exhibition contained notes, drafts of stories, and other documents from the reporters Watergate break-in investigation. I was contracted by The Harry Ransom Center to design what the HRC referred to as a "web exhibition" to promote the opening of the actual exhibition.

Skills Used

UI Design, Visual Design, HTML, CSS

Finished Design

Working with the curator of the archive, I designed and built a web site that both promoted the opening of the Watergate Papers and provided a glimpse of what was held within the archive.
