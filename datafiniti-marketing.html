Datafiniti Marketing Site

Datafiniti’s marketing site served to inform and sell potential customers on Datafiniti’s services. The marketing site offered details about the types of data the company sold, as well as pricing and company information.

Skills Used

Branding, UI Design, Visual Design, HTML, CSS, Google Analytics

My Role

As the in-house design for Datafiniti, I worked with the CEO and head of marketing to design the UI and visual design for the site. I also contributed ideas for types of content as well as ways of increasing the number of leads the site generates. Along with my design duties, I handled all HTML and CSS for the site and set up Google Analytics and KissMetrics to track events and conversions.

Project Goals

Design an aesthetic that was clean, user friendly, and on brand by working with internal stakeholders (CEO and head of marketing). The new site would serve to:
  • Inform potential customers of Datafiniti's products and services
  • Generate sales leads
  • Be quick and easy to read through
  • Be responsive and work as well on mobile devices as it would on desktop computers

Initial Design Concepts

I created several home page designs that demonstrated possible directions for the visual design. In addition to the overall site designs, I also created all graphics used. I reviewed the design concepts with stakeholders at several points during the design process. After much discussion, a design style was chosen.

Finished Design

Shown here are screen-shots of the finished Datafiniti marketing site. The design that was chosen was minimal and worked well with the company logo. The color scheme was also drawn largely from colors used in the logo. I worked with the head of marketing to make sure the design worked with the content and vice versa, in some cases setting copy lengths for each content block. I added a lead form at the bottom of each page to encourage potential customers to contact the company. The site loaded quickly regardless of device or bandwidth speed and worked well across different screen sizes.
