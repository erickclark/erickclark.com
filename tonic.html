A Tonic to the Imagination

A Tonic to the Imagination was another web exhibition designed for the Harry Ransom Center, part of the University of Texas at Austin. The site was built to promote the center’s collection of costume designs by B. J. Simmons & Co, which created costumes for stage and film from 1878 to 1969.

Finished Design

I was contracted by the Harry Ransom Center, to design a brochure site within the HRC's larger site. Shown here are the finished design. The design of the site is based on the costume designs themselves.
