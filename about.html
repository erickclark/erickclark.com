About

Over the last 25 years, I’ve worked as a UX designer for a mix of companies including design agencies, large corporations and startups. The interfaces I've designed include network administration interfaces, advertising dashboards and database search tools. I have a lot of experience designing products for technical users or taking technical workflows and making them user friendly for non-technical users.

My process is user-centered, incorporating feedback from interviews, user tests and A/B tests. The interfaces I create are responsive and are designed to work well across multiple types of devices. My deliverables have included wireframes, visual design comps, process flows and HTML prototypes. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss my qualifications with your team.
